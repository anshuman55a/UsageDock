# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Participation in the UsageDock project should remain respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- assume good intent
- give specific technical feedback
- keep disagreements focused on the work
- respect different levels of experience
- avoid performative hostility

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary language
- doxxing or publishing private information
- disruptive behavior that prevents productive collaboration
- bad-faith escalation or repeated antagonism

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or participants that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately instead of escalating in public threads when possible.
